# Privacy Policy

IMOK Family respects your privacy.

This application may request access to the following permissions:

* Camera: Used only for scanning QR codes to bind family members.
* Location: Used to display real-time location of elderly users.
* Notifications: Used to send alerts such as SOS, fall detection, and inactivity warnings.

We do not sell, share, or rent personal data to third parties.

User data (such as device identifiers and location data) is stored securely using Firebase services for the sole purpose of providing core app functionality.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

If you have any questions, please contact us.
